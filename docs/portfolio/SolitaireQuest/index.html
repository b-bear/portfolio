Solitaire Quest ​

Software: Photoshop, Figma, Z-Brush, Blender, Unity ​

Solitaire Quest was a prototype mobile game, where the player guided Misty the Cat home by playing games of Solitaire. As lead artist, I was responsible for icon & UI design, character design, [3D artwork](../3D/index) & [animation](../AnimationFX/index). Additional logo & scenery design by [Jules Langran](https://www.instagram.com/julesrgb/)
