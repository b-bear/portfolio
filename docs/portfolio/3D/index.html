Green Gardens ​

Software: Z-Brush, Blender & Photoshop ​

I was responsible for character modelling in Z-Brush, Low-poly retopology & texturing for this project. Character Design by Patrick Romano & Polina Mozgovaya.

Solitaire Quest is a prototype mobile game, where the player guided Misty the Cat home by playing games of Solitaire. As lead artist, I was responsible for icon & UI design, character design, 3D artwork & animation
